About the Founder : Dr. Sushil S. Savant

Dr. Sushil S. Savant, a visionary in the field of healthcare, founded this hospital with a mission to make high-quality, compassionate healthcare accessible to all. With a deep commitment to improving public health, Dr. Savant has dedicated his career to serving underserved communities and advancing healthcare standards.

Founding Achievements

  • Established the region's first comprehensive community health program.
  • Secured substantial funding to expand hospital facilities and services.
  • Played a key role in advocating for patient rights and healthcare reforms nationwide.

Specialty

Specializes in Public Health and Community Medicine, with expertise in healthcare policy and an extensive background in community-based health solutions.

Experience

Over 25 years of experience in healthcare, including roles as a senior hospital administrator, policy advisor, and community health leader.

Awards and Achievements

  • Recipient of the National Healthcare Leadership Award (2015).
  • Honored with the Excellence in Community Health Initiatives Award (2018).
  • Named “Healthcare Pioneer of the Year” by Health Magazine (2020).
